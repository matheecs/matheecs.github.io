**编译方法**

```bash
$ hdiutil create -size 20g -type SPARSE -fs "Case-sensitive HFS+" -volname OpenWrt OpenWrt.sparseimage
$ hdiutil attach OpenWrt.sparseimage
$ cd /Volumes/OpenWrt
```
